# Madhav E-Commerce Sales Dashboard – Power BI
-Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a clear and interactive overview of e-commerce sales performance.
It helps understand sales, profit, quantity sold, customer behavior, and category-wise performance at a glance.

The dashboard is designed for business users and management to quickly analyze performance and make data-driven decisions.

-Objective

Track overall sales and profit

Analyze product categories and sub-categories

Understand customer contribution

Identify monthly profit trends

Compare payment methods and states

- Key KPIs (Top Cards)

Total Sales Amount: 23K

Total Profit: 1,958

Total Quantity Sold: 290

Total Orders (Row Count): 6,664

These KPIs give a quick summary of business performance.

-Dashboard Visuals Explanation
1️⃣ Sales by State

Bar chart showing total sales amount across states

Helps identify top-performing regions

2️⃣ Sales by Customer Name

Displays which customers generate the most revenue

Useful for customer segmentation and retention strategy

3️⃣ Quantity by Category

Donut chart showing sales quantity split by product category

Easy comparison of category demand

4️⃣ Quantity by Payment Mode

Donut chart representing payment method usage (COD, UPI, Card, etc.)

Helps understand customer payment preferences

5️⃣ Profit by Sub-Category

Bar chart displaying profit contribution of each sub-category

Useful to identify high and low-profit products

6️⃣ Profit by Month

Monthly profit comparison (January, February, December)

Highlights seasonal trends and business growth

-Filters & Interactivity

State slicer allows filtering data by state

All visuals are interactive and connected

Selecting any visual updates the entire dashboard

-Tools & Technologies Used

Power BI Desktop

Data Cleaning & Modeling

DAX Measures

Interactive Visualizations

-Key Insights

Certain states contribute higher sales volume

A few customers generate major revenue

Some sub-categories are high sales but low profit

Payment preferences vary across customers

-Conclusion

This dashboard provides a complete business overview in one screen.
It is easy to understand, visually clean, and decision-oriented, making it suitable for sales analysis, management reporting, and interviews.
